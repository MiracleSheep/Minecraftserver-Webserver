Modpacks

Occasionally we like to play modded SMPs on the server. We have many different ideas for modpacks. This page is meant for easy access to the modpacks we use on the server, so anyone who wants to join or try them out can just download them from here. Some of the modpacks were unbalanced or boring, so we didin't include them, and others have been lost to time.

1.12.2 Race to the Moon Modpack

This modpack was very fun, although later on it gets a bit grindy. It was inspired from the modpack used in the youtube series known as Race to the Moon, which MiracleSheep liked to watch as a child. Here's a link to the first episode: Minecraft - Race To The Moon - Lets Do This! [1]. Basically, you split into teams, and the first one to the moon wins. This modpack has all of the mods used in the series and a few more. Here is the download:
Download

1.12.2 Ice Age Modpack

This was a challenge modpack. It was inspired from a video by AsianHalfSquat, suggested by Xtreem_Piggy17. Here's a link to the video this modpack was taken from: How To Turn Minecraft Into An Ice Age Survival Game. This version of the modpack has some of the mods in the video removed, since specific mobs were crashing the server. Basically, it is an ice age challenge, meant for hardcore. the main mod is the tough as nails mod, and a mod that makes it constantly winter. It was in hardcore, but it was very difficult, so we gave everyone three lives. Here is the download:
Download

1.12.2 Magic Modpack

This modpack was a meant for an SMP. It is full of magic mods. It is very hard to start out, but becomes much easier as time goes on. It is a very balanced modpack. There are a few optional grindy mods, but the main attractions are the dimension mods, including the Aether, Twighlight forest, Midnight dimension, and Betweenlands. There is also the Ice and Fire mod, which adds dragons and other amazing creatures to the game, which makes starting out difficult. Here is the download:
Download

1.12.2 Avatar Modpack

This is a modified version of a modpack from curseforge. It is themed around the show Avatar, with lots of magic mods and includes mods to help with kingdom building like npc automation. It is highly balanced and has a focused goal, which is to master your respective bending and build a kingdom. It is recommended that each player starts their own kingdom and go to war with eachother. A good late game mix up would be to allow everyone to choose a second element. Here is the download:
