Who We Are

We are a group of friends who like playing Minecraft. We do everything you can do in Minecraft, from competitive pvp and adventure maps to modded or vanilla SMPs. The MiracleSheep server is owned by MiracleSheep (duh) and is used for many of these Minecraft related activities. This website was created for the purpose of celebrating what we've done and letting others see our creations. This page on the website is here to share some memories we've had on the server. Try clicking on each image below to see a short description!
