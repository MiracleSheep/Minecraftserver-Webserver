What is the server?

This website is based around a community minecraft server.

It's simply called MiracleSheep's minecraft server, and it's used for everything minecraft.

We do both vanilla and modded minecraft multiplayer.

We also do the occasional event which can involve hardcore, mods, plugins, and adventure maps.
